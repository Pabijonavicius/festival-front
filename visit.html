Visiting The Festival

Festival

  • Rizzenados is an electronic dance music festival held in Denver, USA. Rizzenados was first held in 2005 and has since become one of the world's largest and most notable music festivals. It now stretches over 2 weekends and it usually sells out in minutes.

Directions

  1. Firstly, come to the city of Denver.
  2. Navigate to the Monaghann Forest, then you should see the Rizzenados Flag, there you will meet our workers, which will give you directions to the camping area.
  3. You will be provided a locker, for your important documents.
  4. You must wear a hand wirst all the time, penalties may be given if you won't follow this.
  5. You must give back locker keys, when festival will be finished.

Camping

  • Everyone in your campsite must have a valid festival admission wristband.
  • All of your belongings, RV/trailer slide outs and vehicles must fit within the dimensions of your site. Most sites are 18 x 40.
  • Noise curfews apply to all campgrounds.
  • All wristbands and campsite vehicle hang tags will be shipped to you in advance of the festival. It is your responsibility to arrive with all necessary wristbands and hang tags in order to gain entry to the festival and its campgrounds.
  • Absolutely no motorized vehicles.
